Build stuff. Shape it however you want. Make it yours.

Place nodes, sculpt glowing vectors, and create cool shapes in this super precise puzzle game where you create shapes.

FEATURES

  • Intuitive Node Placement

    Drag nodes onto the canvas and instantly connect them with clean vector lines you can tweak.

  • Real-Time Form Sculpting

    Bend, stretch, and refine your structures with smooth touch controls that feel great to use.

  • Hit the Target

    Make your shapes fit the goals - sometimes they rotate, sometimes you need to hit energy levels, stuff like that.

  • Creative & Challenge Modes

    Switch between relaxing freeform building and puzzles that get harder as you go, with star ratings to track your progress.

HOW IT WORKS

  • 1. Place Your Nodes

    Position core points across the grid to define your structure’s foundation.

  • 2. Sculpt the Vectors

    Draw and adjust glowing lines to form stable, beautiful geometric shapes.

  • 3. Complete the Form

    Align your creation with the level goal, trigger reactions, and watch it come alive.
